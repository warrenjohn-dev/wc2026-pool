How the pool works

This is the first 48-team World Cup, so the format is new. The 48 teams are split into 12 groups of four. Each team plays the other three in its group once. You earn points by correctly predicting how far teams go — from the group stage all the way to the champion.

Who advances: the top two teams from every group go through automatically (24 teams), plus the eight best third-place teams across all 12 groups. That makes 32 teams in the knockout Round of 32, then it is single-elimination: Round of 32 → Round of 16 → Quarter-finals → Semi-finals → Final.

★ STEP 2 · WILDCARD ROUND — pick 8 of the 12 third-place teams 0 / 8

There are 12 third-place teams (one per group) but only 8 Round-of-32 spots left for them — the other 24 go to the group winners and runners-up. So FIFA ranks all 12 third-place teams against each other and the top 8 advance; the bottom 4 are eliminated. They're ranked by points, then goal difference, then goals scored. Pick that team for each group below, then tick the 8 you think finish with the best records — your 8 picks auto-fill the “3rd” slots in the bracket.
